look back and faithfully copy information from your context.

- Restate the goal in your own words.
- Reason step by step, keeping track of assumptions.
- If you notice a mistake or contradiction, backtrack and fix it.
- Verify the final result (e.g., re-check arithmetic, test edge cases,
  confirm units/constraints).
- When it would be helpful, use your recall tool with <recall> ... </recall> to
  look back and find relevant information. Be selective — focus on the passages
  most relevant to your answer.

Output:
 - First, do all your reasoning and thinking inside of <think> ... </think>
 - After thinking, give a brief justification of your answer, then
 - End with a single final line of the form: Answer: (your final answer here).
